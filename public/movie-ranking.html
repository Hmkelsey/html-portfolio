Hannah's Favorite Movies

My Top Movies of All Time

Far and Away

It's just a very romantic film, has fun comedy to it, good drama, and has terrific pictures and sights.

Braveheart

It is a sweeping epic story with excellent musical scored and a cinematography that is second to none. It's scenery is beautifully captured.

Pride and Prejudice

Stunning cinematography, a dazzling score, a phenomenal cast and a timeless story...it doesn't get any better than this! Endlessly re-watchable and an instant classic.
